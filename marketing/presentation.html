The Quantum Threat to Video Calls

Today's encrypted video calls use RSA/ECC algorithms that quantum computers will break. Adversaries are already harvesting encrypted traffic — the "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" attack.

⚠️ The Risk

  • RSA-2048 & ECC vulnerable to Shor's algorithm
  • Quantum computers expected to break them by 2030
  • Nation-state actors harvesting encrypted call traffic
  • Video calls contain the most sensitive discussions
  • Once broken, all recorded calls are exposed forever

  • Dilithium-5 + Ed25519 signatures for all signaling
  • E2EE media frames — SFU never sees plaintext
